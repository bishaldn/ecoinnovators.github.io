Assamese Handwritten Character and Digit Recognition System


Assamese , which is actually known as Asamiya is a state language spoken in the state of Assam, Meghalaya, Arunachal Pradesh, and also in Bengladesh & Bhutan. Nearly 15 to 20 million people speak the language of Assamese. There are lot of similarities between Bengali and the Assamese language except for a few differences.


In this work, we have created our own Assamese digit & Character dataset, built a model to detect or predict Assamese Character & Digit. User can upload images to predict each digit's and character's original class. Datasets will be uploaded soon.......!!
